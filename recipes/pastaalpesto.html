Pasta al Pesto

What is Pasta al Pesto?

Pesto is a sauce that traditionally consists of crushed garlic, European pine nuts, coarse salt, basil leaves, and hard cheese such as Parmigiano-Reggiano or Pecorino Sardo, all blended with olive oil.

Ingredients

  • linquine
  • cashews
  • pine nuts
  • fresh basil
  • garlic clove
  • lemon juice
  • salt
  • extra virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta

  2. Blend the nuts first

  3. Make the basil pesto by adding to the blender

  4. blend

  5. combine the pesto and pasta and mix
